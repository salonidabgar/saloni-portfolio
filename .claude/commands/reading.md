## Context

Saloni reads philosophy, psychology, evolution, business, and spirituality. She connects everything back to her work in embedded systems, her fitness practice, and her quest to understand human nature. Her blog ("Thinking") has an immersive reading experience with gradient drop caps, pull quotes, and highlighted text.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Ask what format they want

Ask the user to pick one:
- **Tweet** — A single tweet (≤280 chars). Punchy takeaway + personal reflection.
- **Thread** — A 5-8 tweet thread. Multiple takeaways or one idea unpacked.
- **Essay** — A full essay in `content/blog/`. For deep dives, end-of-book reflections, or connecting a book's ideas to Saloni's life.

### Step 2: Generate the content

**For Tweets:**
- Format: "[Takeaway]. [Saloni's 1-2 sentence reflection connecting to her life]. — from [Book] by [Author]"
- No hashtag spam. Max 1-2 if natural.
- Authentic > viral

**For Threads:**
- Tweet 1: Hook — "Reading [Book] by [Author]. [One line about what hit hardest] 🧵"
- Tweets 2-6: One takeaway per tweet. Each connects to Saloni's world: JLR, yoga, fitness, philosophy, nature
- Final tweet: Closing thought + "What are you reading?"
- Each tweet works alone AND in sequence

**For Essays:**
- Follow the `/blog` skill instructions exactly (see blog.md)
- Use category "Personal Growth" or "Philosophy"
- **The intersection rule:** Connect every reading insight to at least one of: embedded systems, fitness, nature, psychology, yoga, or the experience of building things
- Use `> blockquotes` for the most powerful book passages
- Use `*italics*` for Saloni's personal reflections on those passages
- Structure: personal hook → 3-5 key ideas with lived connections → closing reflection

### Voice guidelines
- First person, conversational, no academic tone
- Connect every lesson to real life — firmware at JLR, deadlifts, badminton, meditation, a walk in nature
- Be honest about what resonated and what challenged existing beliefs
- Short paragraphs. **Bold the key lines.** End with an *italicized reflection.*
